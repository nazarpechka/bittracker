Cryptocurrency portfolio tracker

What is BitTracker?

BitTracker allows you to follow your cryptocurrency portfolio status automatically, combining data from a variety of cryptocurrency exchanges and your manual input.

Track your current balance

You can see your current investments value in a fiat currency of choice, with pretty and informative graphs and real-time data on prices from CoinMarketCap.

How to start

To start using the app, press the "Login" button, then press "Don't have an account?" button. There you should provide your actual information, and then you can start using the app by providing API keys to exchanges or manually inputting your crypto holdings.
